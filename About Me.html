About Me




I am a video editor for CUSTOM HIGHLIGHT VIDEOS, a game-film recruitment service helping high school, college, and professional athletes showcase their skills. I used to edit my videos with Final Cut Pro (FCP), but have transitioned to Premiere Pro. FCP is a strong editing software that will work well for editing any videos you may need to work on, but Premiere Pro allows the user to get much more creative with their edits, and adjust the way your video is exported. I edit about 20-30 hours per week, and know the importance of being comfortable with your editing software.
