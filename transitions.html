Which Software Is Best For You?




Final Cut Pro vs. Premiere Pro

Final Cut Pro

There is not much to explain here-Final Cut Pro come pre-loaded with the basic transitions like gradually fading and wipes, but you can't really make your own. You can purchase additional transitions online, but your creativity is certainly limited.

Premiere Pro

Transitions may be my personal favorite part of Premiere Pro. It does come with some pre-loaded oes like Final Cut, but with Premiere Pro, you as a creator are truly given a chance to 'WOW' the viewer of your content by making your own transitions. My favorite transition style to work with is using the easy-ease tool to wipe footage in naturally.

Choosing between the two softwares here come down to timing. Final Cut is a super simple drag-and-drop system, but limits you. Premiere Pro lets you do whatever you want, but it takes a while.
